About Me

With a diverse background in banking, customer service, and warehouse management, I bring a unique perspective to the world of data analytics. Throughout my career, I've leveraged my skills to streamline processes and optimize performance, whether it was assisting in managing an Amazon account for a medical equipment warehouse during the pandemic or providing top-notch service to banking clients.

I hold an Associate's degree in Business Administration and have furthered my education with a certificate in both Data Analytics and Full-Stack Web Development.

After I acquired the Data Analytics certificate, I would often assist students with problems they were facing. With my continuous desire to help people succeed being noticed, I was offered a TA position from CourseCareers, where I can help students succeed and grow their skills in Excel, Tableau, SQL, and Python.

Driven by curiosity and a dedication to continuous learning, I am excited to apply my skills and knowledge to extract insights, drive informed decisions, and make meaningful contributions to any team or project I'm involved in.
